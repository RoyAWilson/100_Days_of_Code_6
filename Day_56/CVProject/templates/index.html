A little about me:

A man living in the South East of England. Home being in the Garden of England, Kent I was born in Folkestone on Kent coast, not far from Dover, in the days when there were still sailings every day over to Boulogne in France. I moved with my Family to Maidstone in 1979 following my father's work. My grandparents and my mother's siblings were also living in Maistone. I completed the last few years of secondary education in a local comprehensive.
After finishing schooling I worked on local farms picking and packing fruits and driving tractors. My first proper job was as a builders labourer working on various sites around the area including working on the new
(back then) conference centre on Brighton seafront soon after the bombing of Grand Hotel. In later years I worked on several fairly pretigious sites in and around London as a tower crane operator.
When the building went bust, I re-skilled to work in offices gaining my Level 3 in Business Adminitstration.
I enjoy reading fiction and walking, when the weather is good and am an animal lover having had both pet dogs, cats and fish
